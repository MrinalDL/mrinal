সম্প্ৰীতিৰ ফুল

"সম্প্ৰীতিৰ ফুল" means "Flower of Harmony". The poem is about the metaphorical idea of a flower representing harmony, peace, and mutual understanding. It emphasizes the beauty and value of harmonious relationships or unity, suggesting that it is something precious and nurtured like a flower.

পৰীক্ষাৰ প্ৰেমিকজন

The poem "পৰীক্ষাৰ প্ৰেমিকজন" refers to the dedication of an aspirant who is preparing for job exams. Metaphorically, it shows that the boy has fallen in love with the exams, to the point that he cannot escape from the preparation.
